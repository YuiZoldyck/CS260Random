About the Photographer


This website is created by Yin-Chen You who is from Taipei, Taiwan. She is currently a student at BYU studying for her bachelor's degree. She really misses Taiwan and hopes to go back sometimes soon. Her purpose for this website is to help more people understand the beauty of her home country. If you ever want to know more about Taiwan, reach out to her!!
